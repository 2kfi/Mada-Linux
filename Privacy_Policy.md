# MadaOS Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 23/2/2026_

## 1. Overview

MadaOS respects user privacy.

MadaOS is based on Arch Linux. Package updates and repositories are provided by Arch Linux infrastructure and are subject to their respective policies.

MadaOS does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data.

The operating system includes no telemetry, analytics, tracking services, or background data collection mechanisms.

## 2. Third-Party Applications

MadaOS includes third-party open-source applications (such as web browsers, office software, and media players). These applications may communicate with their respective developers for updates, online services, or security features.

Such applications operate under their own privacy policies and data practices.

Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of included third-party software.

## 3. Changes to This Policy

If MadaOS ever introduces features that involve data collection, this policy will be updated accordingly and published in the official repository.
